Demo payment gateway

Fake checkout flow with a real product-style UI.

This page simulates a Razorpay-style payment experience for college and demo purposes. It supports UPI and Card choices, stores payment data in MySQL, and shows a generated transaction ID.

UPI option Shows a QR-style block and demo UPI handle for mock scanning flow.
Card option Collects fake card values for a realistic payment popup experience.
MySQL storage On success, the payment is stored in the `payment_demo.payments` table.
